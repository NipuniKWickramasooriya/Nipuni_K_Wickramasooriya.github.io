Introducing the University of Vavuniya's Learning Management System (LMS):

Our project is all about creating an easy-to-use online platform for the University of Vavuniya. We've used some cool technology to make it happen: Figma for designing how it looks, React.js for making it work smoothly, Spring Boot for handling the behind-the-scenes stuff, and MySQL for storing all the important data.

With our LMS, students, lectures, and administrators can log in using their own usernames and passwords. Once logged in, students can check their grades, download result sheets, see course details, and stay updated with campus news. Administrators have the power to add results, courses, news, and create accounts for users and tlectures. Lectures can also do their part by adding assignments, papers, materials, and references to help students learn better.
